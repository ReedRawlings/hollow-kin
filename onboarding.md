# **Hollow Kin — UI/UX & Player Information Design**

*Working Document — Subject to Change*

---

## **Overview**

Hollow Kin has a lot of overlapping systems — traits, marks, abilities, stars, longevity, breeding, capture, relics, and town upgrades. The player needs clear, consistent ways to parse all of it without being overwhelmed. This document covers the onboarding flow to introduce the player to each composite piece of the game.

---
## **Onboarding Flow**

The game walks players through the game in two phases.
Phase 1: Selecting Creatures for a run, Combat, Capture, Rest, Leveling
Phase 2: Traits, Longevity, Breeding

## Phase 1

* Initially player only see's one button on the screen "Ascend" clicking it takes them into the tower, but instead of starting a typical run they encounter the old man
* Player encounters an old man who offers to help them ascent the tower. He presents them with the option of two sets of three different creatures outlined in the creature-roster.md file
* Old man then battles the player with the three other creatures. Upon defeating the old man the player gets enough exp to level up twice, fully heals MP and HP
* Old man gives the player some plasm and sends them into their first tower run
* On first encounter old man suggest the player try to capture one of the creatures they battle with the plasm he gave them. This always works. This introduces capturing and the inventory system to the player
* Players second event is always a rest to show the player can heal MP or HP to full
* Player phases two more encounters unlocking traits on at least one of their active pets. If the player decided to swap the captured pet with one of their starts it would not have its traits yet.


## Phase 2
* After the three total encounters players will face a mini-boss. If they defeat it it will unlock a mark for their active and living pets. 
* Whether they win or lose against the mini-boss the run will end. Old man will instruct the player that the longevity for their pets ticked down and they'll need to either breed or retire their pets
* Retire option will be greyed out and old man will want the player to breed two of the creatures with a trait walking them through the breeding system (how tiers pass down, marks optionally, and getting a new pet)
* This will leave the player with three pets, one bred starting with a tier unlocked, one captured pet, and the final being the third pet from the starting trio

## Finish Onboarding
* From here the leathersmith will appear as well as the breeding box and the player can interact with them or immediately start another run.
